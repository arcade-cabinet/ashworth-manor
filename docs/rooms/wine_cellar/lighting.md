## Design Notes
- Torches only. More aggressive flicker than candles — random spikes.
- Ambient darkness 0.85. Barely visible beyond torch reach.
- Wine bottle glass catches torch light — glinting in the dark.
- Acoustically dead. Sounds absorbed by stone. Player's own sounds feel swallowed.
