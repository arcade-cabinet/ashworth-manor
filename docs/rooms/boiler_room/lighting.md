# Boiler Room — Lighting

### 1. Boiler Glow (OmniLight3D)
| Position | (0, 0.5, 3) | Color | `(1.0, 0.4, 0.1)` — deep industrial red-orange | Energy | 2.0 | Range | 6.0 | Shadows | Yes | Flickering | **Yes** — slow industrial pulse |

This is NOT cozy fire. This is industrial heat. Different from parlor fireplace — aggressive, mechanical.

### Design Notes
- Metal grate floor catches light from below as well — boiler glow bleeds through grate
- Pipe shadows on ceiling are prominent
- Ambient darkness 0.7 but the boiler creates a strong directional pool
