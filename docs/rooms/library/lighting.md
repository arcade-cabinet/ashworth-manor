## Design Notes
- Dark wood paneling absorbs light heavily. The room feels dimmer than its 0.5 ambient_darkness.
- Three warm sources but all modest — scholarly shadow, reading by candlelight.
- Globe area (-3, 3) is at the edge of light — you have to step into shadow to find the key.
