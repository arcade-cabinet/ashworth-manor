# Attic Storage

**Room ID:** `attic_storage`
**Floor:** Attic (+2)

---

## Narrative Purpose
Elizabeth's prison. Where she lived, drew, played with her doll, and wrote letters that were never sent. The declaration now carries the whole childhood-remains read the room needs: portrait, doll chair and candle, trunk, cot and dragged mattress, loose drawings, a dead lamp, estate junk, and the hidden way toward her inner sanctum.

This is the most narratively dense room in the game.
