## Narrative Purpose
Elizabeth's prison. Where she lived, drew, played with her doll, and wrote letters that were never sent. Contains: her portrait (eyes painted over), the porcelain doll (with hidden key inside), her unsent letter, and the hidden door to her inner sanctum.

This is the most narratively dense room in the game.
