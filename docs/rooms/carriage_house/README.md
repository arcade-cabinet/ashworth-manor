# Carriage House

**Room ID:** `carriage_house`
**Floor:** Grounds (exterior)

---

## Narrative Purpose
Service outbuilding, not just a storage shed. It holds the duplicate portrait with the cellar key taped behind the frame, the evidence of a servant sleeping out here instead of inside the house, and the practical clutter of a working carriage side that has been abandoned in place.

The carriage house belongs to the estate's service-side circulation, not the ceremonial front approach. Its access should feel like backstage discovery: a route servants knew, not guests.
