## Narrative Purpose

The grand entrance hall. Two stories high, grand staircase, chandelier. This is where the Ashworth family made their first impression — wealth, taste, authority. Now it stands frozen, a museum to a family that vanished in the night.

The foyer establishes:
- **The frozen moment** — clock stopped at 3:33, no ticking
- **The patriarch** — Lord Ashworth's portrait, "Rites of Passage" book
- **The supernatural** — mirror that moves independently
- **Player agency** — light switch controls chandelier
- **Hub access** — connects to all ground floor rooms + stairs up
