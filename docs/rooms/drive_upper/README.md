## Narrative Purpose

`drive_upper` is the forecourt pull.

It should communicate:
- the mansion now has social weight
- statuary and widened paving signal ceremony and class
- the player is almost committed to the front door
- the estate is about to reveal more than one route, but has not done so yet

## Tableau Thesis

The player should feel the approach widening just enough to become theatrical. Hedges open, statues flank the route, and warm light from the front steps begins to register against the colder exterior.

What the frame should do:
- keep the front steps as the thesis
- use statues and hedge breaks as framing, not clutter
- make the manor feel closer and more judgmental
- preserve enough withholding that the forecourt still lands as a reveal
