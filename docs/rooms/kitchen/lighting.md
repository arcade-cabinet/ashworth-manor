# Kitchen — Lighting

## Light Sources

### 1. Hearth (OmniLight3D) — PRIMARY

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Position | (0, 0.5, 4) — north wall, in hearth |
| Color | `(1.0, 0.6, 0.3)` — deep ember orange |
| Energy | 1.2 |
| Range | 6.0 |
| Shadows | Yes |
| Flickering | **Yes** — very slow dying pulse |

**Note:** This hearth is DEAD. The light is extremely dim — barely embers. The flicker is the last gasp of a fire that died in 1891.

## Lighting Design Notes

- **Darkest ground floor room.** Ambient darkness 0.6. The kitchen was for servants, not show. Less invested in illumination.
- **Dying hearth only warm source.** Everything feels cold and utilitarian.
- **Transition to basement darkness.** This room prepares the eye for the basement below — it's the first step into dim.
- **Stone floor reflects differently** than marble — less specular, more diffuse. Light dies sooner.
