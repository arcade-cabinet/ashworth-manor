# Attic Stairwell — Dialogue

```
~ stairwell_debris
	Gravel and plaster dust on the stairs. Your footprints are the first in years.
	
	Each step leaves a mark. Proof you were here.


~ stairwell_wall
	The plaster is cracked and stained. Water damage from above — the attic roof leaks.
	
	Through the largest crack, you see bare wooden lath. And scratches on the wood. From the other side. Fingernail marks.
```
