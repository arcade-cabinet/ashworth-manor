## Narrative Purpose
Dead winter garden with one impossible living white lily — Elizabeth's life force reaching beyond the house. This is the rear-estate hinge: a formal family garden gone cold, reached from the mansion's service side and branching onward to the chapel, greenhouse, and locked crypt path.
