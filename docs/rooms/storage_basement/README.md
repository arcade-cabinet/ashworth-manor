## Narrative Purpose

First descent into darkness. The family stored what they didn't want seen — old furniture, forgotten portraits, evidence of the daughter they erased. The scratched family portrait is the first physical proof of Elizabeth's erasure, and the room now also reads as the estate's service hinge: ugly storage, hidden circulation, and the first believable route toward the carriage side of the property.
