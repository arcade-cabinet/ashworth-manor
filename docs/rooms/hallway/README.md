## Narrative Purpose

The spine of the upper floor. A narrow corridor connecting bedrooms, library, and guest room. At the far end: the locked attic door — the goal that drives Act II.

The hallway establishes:
- **The locked door** — the attic is forbidden. Player needs a key.
- **The children** — a painting shows three children. But there were four.
- **Transition** — from public ground floor to private upper floor. The family's secrets live here.
- **Vertical access** — stairs down to foyer, locked door up to attic.
