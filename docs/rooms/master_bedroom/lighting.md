## Lighting Design Notes

- **Intimate gloom.** Two candles only — just enough to read the diary, not enough to feel safe.
- **Prussian blue walls absorb warm light** — the candles fight against the cold walls.
- **Mirror reflection** — the candle near the mirror creates a doubled light effect. When the player examines the mirror, the candle behind them makes their silhouette visible.
- **Moonlight through window** — cold contrast. The outside world exists but feels distant.
- **After `elizabeth_aware`:** Candles gutter more frequently. Brief moments of near-darkness.
